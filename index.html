STARFIGHT

You will need Piloting, Systems, Weapons, and Cool to in order to Starfight.

Starfight unlocks Missions and generates Legacy to become an Epic Starfighter Pilot.
PILOTING

Put time in Piloting to build up more Skill.

Having lots of Skill lets you do more things with your Starfighter.
SYSTEMS

Outfit your Starfighter with advanced Systems to increase your Power.

You won't get far with limited Power, so work on improving your systems.
WEAPONS

Build stronger Weapons to increase your Multiplier to everything.

Multiplier allows you to outlast your enemies and fight longer fights. Now fight.
COOL

You'll need Cool a bit later, but it's how you get your Boost.

In order to Defeat Enemies, you'll have to build up your Leadership.
DEFEAT ENEMIES

In order to Defeat Enemies:
- Min/max your Skills
- Retrofit your ships
- Become a true Leader

Only then, can you begin your Legacy and unlock Missions.
FLEETS and LEADERSHIP

You used your old parts to build a Fleet! Retrofit to update your Weapons and Systems.

You'll find that you need strong Leadership to command well.
